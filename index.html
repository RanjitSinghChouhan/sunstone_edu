Eligibility Criteria

  1. candidate must have completed their graduation with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for sc/st category) or an equivalent cgpa in any discipline from a recognized university.
  2. candidates currently in their final year of graduation can also apply. in that case, the admission offered will be provisional till the time graduation documents are not received.
  3. candidates must have appeared in any one of the following entrance exams - cat, mat, cmat, atma,xat, gmat and state entrances.

Admission Process

since this is a post-graduate program, a candidate needs to be a graduate already or completing their graduation by the end of the current academic year from a ugc or aicte recognised university or college.

  1. submit application form
    the candidate can visit our website (https://sunstone.edu.in) and fill out the application form by clicking on the “apply now” button. the candidate needs to submit the required details along with supporting documents which are class 10th marksheet, class 12th marksheet, graduation certificate, graduation marksheet and a valid government photo id proof.
  2. appear for admission test
    after filling out the application form, you are required to appear for the sunstone eduversity admissions test by scheduling the same. the s.e.a.t is an online test designed to assess your general aptitude, verbal ability and psychometric profile.
  3. personal interview
    based on the s.e.a.t. scores and your profile, a personal interview shall be arranged. during the interview, your soft skills, aptitude and grit will be assessed, along with your motivation to pursue a management degree.
  4. application review
    your complete profile will now be reviewed by the admissions committee. the decision of your application acceptance/rejection will be communicated to you over the registered email address and the postal address provided.
  5. admission status
    candidates will be required to accept the admission offer and confirm their seat within the given timeline by completing the steps mentioned in the offer letter.

For any query during the process, you can email us at admissions@sunstone.edu.in or call us at 7065303030.
